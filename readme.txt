== Installation ==
**Easy Product Recommendations Addon for WooCommerce Installation Process in your WordPress Dashboard:**

**Step -1:** Upload the Product Recommendations Addon for WooCommerce to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory and click `Install Now`

**Step -2:** Activate the plugin through the `Plugins` menu in WordPress

**Step -3:** Now, a new menu called `Product Recommendations` will appear on your WooCommerce Menu. From there, you can create and manage Product Recommendations Addon for WooCommerce.

**Easy Recommendation Engines Process Using Product Recommendations Addon for WooCommerce:**

**Step -1:** Hover on the Product Recommendations icon on your menu and click on `Create New`

**Step -2:** Give your engine title.

**Step -3:** Select your engine type.
